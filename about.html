OUR MISSION

VABRYON is a global-minded entity focused on researching and acquiring the essentials of survival arts. We integrate various martial arts, parkour, and freerunning into one unified flow of operational and tactical expertise, providing comprehensive guidance to all our movement members. Access to this specialized knowledge is our primary mean of spreading these critical skills and the true understanding behind them.

ELITE COLLABORATION

Not only led by the founders, but strengthened by collaboration with elite performers and top-tier professionals including MMA trainers and world-class parkour athletes. Each art taught by masters from its original birthplace. This movement gathers all the missing pieces of the survival arts puzzle with a realism edge.

PREPARE FOR ANYTHING

Here you will learn, develop, and refine your mental and physical state in terms of self-defense and survival to be prepared for whatever that may take place in the near or distant future. Master the essential arts you're passionate about and get ready to handle any situation.

We first entered this field in 2016. As passionate individuals, we developed this vision and decided it deserves to be shared with the world. Together, we shape the armor of security for our members, their families, belongings, and everyone around us.
